Various Sentiments Pie Chart

The pie chart depicts the percentage of each sentiment score in all the reviews. There are four types of sentiments derived from the sentiment score, Negative (sentiment score from 1 to 2), Not Bad (sentiment score from 2 to 3), Neutral (sentiment score from 3 to 4), Positive (from 4 to 5). This pie chart helps to determine the proportion of each sentiment in each of the reviews, so that you can judge what sentiment dominates the reviews that you get.
